Seed your blog articles with tags automatically.

== Description ==
Save time and do better SEO with powerful automatic tagging. Tag Seeder will automatically tag your articles based on your settings and the content.

== Demo and Tutorial ==
[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qbdpZpMkAwc]

Take a look at some of its key features:
* Checks for minimum and maximum matches in post's titles and content.
* Adds tags automatically to your new posts.


== Installation =

1. Upload the `WP TagSeeder` folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin using the `Plugins` menu in WordPress.
3. When you log in the first time, please provide your name and email id to receive the activation code.
4. The process is complete.
